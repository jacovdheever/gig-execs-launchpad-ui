# Business Update: November 17, 2025

## Executive Summary

This update covers significant enhancements to our gig and bid management system, making it easier for professionals to find and apply for opportunities, and for clients to review and manage bids. We've also improved security processes to ensure safe deployments.

---

## üéØ Key Improvements for Professionals

### Better Gig Discovery
- **Role Type & Location**: All gigs now show whether they're In-person, Hybrid, or Remote, plus the specific location (or "Fully Remote")
- **Bid Status Tracking**: Professionals can see which gigs they've already bid on with a clear badge
- **Filter Your Bids**: New filter option to show only gigs where you've submitted a bid

### Enhanced Bid Submission
- **Answer Screening Questions**: When submitting a bid, professionals can now answer any screening questions the client has set up
- **Attach Supporting Documents**: Professionals can upload examples of work, detailed proposals, or other supporting documentation with their bid
- **View & Edit Your Bids**: Professionals can see their submitted bid details and make updates if needed

---

## üéØ Key Improvements for Clients

### Better Gig Information
- **Role Type & Location**: Clients can specify whether a role is In-person, Hybrid, or Remote, and add the location
- **Flexible Budgeting**: New "To be confirmed" option for budget when the exact amount isn't finalized yet

### Enhanced Bid Review
- **Bid Count at a Glance**: See how many bids each gig has received directly on the gig listing page
- **Detailed Bid View**: Click on any bid to see:
  - Full proposal text
  - Answers to all screening questions
  - All attached documents (viewable and downloadable)
- **Easy Navigation**: Bids are organized in an accordion format - see summary at a glance, expand for full details
