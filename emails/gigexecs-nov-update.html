Dear GigExecs community,
It's been a while… and for good reason.
We've been building.
As an early-stage startup, we hit the usual bumps: some things worked, others didn't. But your feedback pushed us to rethink, rebuild, and sharpen our focus. The result is a cleaner, simpler and stronger version of the GigExecs platform — designed with you at the centre.
Today, we're excited to share a series of meaningful improvements that make finding and managing flexible work easier, faster, and more transparent for both professionals and clients.

What's New for Professionals?

Smarter Gig Discovery

Every gig now shows whether it's Remote, Hybrid, or In-person, plus the exact location. No more guessing — just faster decisions.

Track Where You've Applied

You'll now see a clear badge for gigs you've already bid on, plus a handy filter to show only your submitted bids.

More Powerful Applications

You can now:
  • Answer screening questions set by clients
  • Attach proposals, supporting documents, or past work
  • View and edit your submitted bids anytime
These changes help you submit higher-quality applications and stand out instantly.

What's New for Clients

Richer Gig Listings

You can now specify role type and location, and even set budget to "To be confirmed" when you're still scoping the role.

Faster, Clearer Bid Review

You'll see how many bids each gig has received at a glance, and can open each bid to review:
  • Full proposal text
  • Answers to screening questions
  • All attached documents
All neatly organised for quicker, more informed decisions.
Explore the new GigExecs experience

A New Addition: External Gigs

We're now listing high-quality gigs from outside the GigExecs network.
These are hand-picked opportunities — and we earn nothing from them.
The goal is simple:
  • More real opportunities.
  • More flexibility.
  • More global access for senior professionals.

Better community engagement

We've added a new Community tab inside our platform where you can engage with fellow GigExecs, network, find interesting news and content from your fellow professionals and clients. We believe that being part of this community matters and we would like to create a space for all of us to connect and engage.

Thank You

Our mission remains bold:
To become the global home for senior talent and for companies embracing flexible, high-quality work.
We believe this model is a win-win — faster access to exceptional experience, without red tape or traditional hiring costs.
We'd love your feedback on the new platform.
What works for you? What can be better?
Every improvement we make begins with your voice.
